TV Shows Recommendations

I won't ramble even though I could easily so heres my some picks avoiding things everyone's probably heard of

  • The Midnight Gospal: On Netflix, interesting for Adult animated show cause the dialog is all from a podcast by the Duncan Trusell with the animation work being done by Adventure Time's Pendleton Ward
  • Love Death + Robots: On Netflix, a collection of various animation styles and short stories. I thought the producers of these animations put a lot of passion in them and still amazes me just how good animation work has become
  • Mob Psycho 100: An Anime, honestly this was one of the funniest shows I've seen in a while plus strangely enough heart wrenching moments. One of my favorites great comedy and great character development.If you are any bit curious please do check it out on CrunchyRoll or other streaming sites for Anime
  • Malcom in the Middle: Yeah its not exactly a niche show, but I myself never heard of it till like a year ago and thought it would be a good show to keep on your radar. It's got an old school feel to it reminding me of a show like Friends and if you every seen Breaking Bad you get to see Bryan Cranston play a polar opposite character in this show, respect to him for having such good acting range.

Indie Game Recommendations

Alright time to get into the good stuff if your itching for new game to dump hours into here's my picks for indie game titles

  • Hades: A fun roguelike with a story that functions as part of the gameplay. You play as Zagreus the son of Hades and are "hell" bent to get out of the Underworld. On your journey up you have to battle the creatures of the underworld traveling through its realms to get to the overworld while being aided by the greek gods of Olympus. Plus you can pet Cerberus which is pretty cool. Great art, fun gameplay, overall a fun masterpiece.
  • Hollow Knight: A MetroidVania style game in which you are a mysterious bug knight entering into a desolate old kingdom of bugs in search of its mysteries. You explore the lush beauty and dark depths of this bug Kingdom in search of answers on what has happened to the kingdom. As you explore clashing with horrid insects and vicious lords of the domain slowly collecting the answers found in the hidden lore of the realm. Love this game I've played through it a couple times
  • Faster Than Light: An old game by now but a good one. You start the game in control of the crew of a Federation spaceship running away from a Rebel Armada seeking to destroy your crew before you can relay vital information to the rest of the Federation. On the journey you encounter various enemy ships from pirates to strange alien creatures while picking up upgrades to make your little space craft become an unstoppable fighting machine. First game that got me into the Indie scene has a special place in my heart
